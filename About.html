About Me 😎

Hi, I'm Prince Moses, an aspiring software developer from Kenya with a passion for creating websites that are both functional and visually appealing. For me, coding isn't just about writing lines of code it's about solving problems, bringing ideas to life, and constantly pushing myself to learn something new.

My passion for technology began in primary school, where I developed a curiosity about computers and how they work. In high school, I turned that curiosity into practical skills by learning Microsoft Word, Spreadsheets (Excel), and Database Management. Today, I continue to build on that foundation by creating modern, responsive websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while constantly learning new technologies to grow as a web developer.

I'm currently sharpening my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, graphics & video design and building projects that challenge me to grow with every step. I enjoy exploring new technologies, experimenting with creative ideas, and turning curiosity into real-world solutions.

When I'm away from my keyboard, you'll usually find me watching football, gaming, or discovering the latest trends in technology. I believe every challenge is an opportunity to improve, and every project is a chance to create something meaningful.
